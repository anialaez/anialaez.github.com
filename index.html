About Me

I didn't start in marketing. I spent over a decade in operations and customer service, working across EMEA for companies like Mattel and Inditex. I was good at it — managing teams, fixing processes, keeping things running across time zones. But I always found myself more interested in why customers behaved the way they did than in the operations themselves.

In 2025 I made the switch. I completed a full-time marketing bootcamp at Ironhack and went straight into managing paid campaigns at a fintech startup. In four months, with a budget of around €3,000, I brought CPA down by 77%, tripled monthly leads, and hit a peak ROAS of 6.65x.

Alongside that, I run content and social media for an Amsterdam-based pet startup, where my articles and Reels have reached over 400,000 people in seven months.

I work in English and Spanish, I'm comfortable with data, and I take campaigns from brief to results without losing the creative thread.
