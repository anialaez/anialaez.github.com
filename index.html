About Me

I didn't take the traditional route into marketing — and honestly, I think that's what makes me different. I spent over a decade working in operations and customer success across EMEA, at companies like Mattel and Stradivarius (Inditex). Managing cross-market teams, coordinating commercial activations, building tracking systems from scratch. That experience gave me something a lot of marketers don't have: I know how a business actually works, and I know what it really means when a campaign performs — or doesn't.

In 2025, I decided to bet on myself. I completed an intensive digital marketing bootcamp at Ironhack — one of the most rigorous programs in the field — and jumped straight into the deep end: managing Google Ads campaigns at Tytle, a fintech startup, with a real budget and real accountability. No hand-holding, no templates. Just data, curiosity, and a lot of iteration.

The results speak for themselves: CPA down 77%, leads up 258%, CPL reduced by 70%, peak ROAS of 6.65x. All within a few months. I'm proud of those numbers — but I'm even prouder of what they represent: that when I commit to something, I figure it out.

I'm based in Amsterdam and looking for fully remote roles in Europe, or hybrid opportunities in the Amsterdam area. I'd love to be part of a team where data genuinely shapes decisions and where there's room to keep growing while doing work that actually matters. If that sounds like your team, I'd really love to hear from you.
