The Sparkle Edit

Case Study

What if planning a celebration could feel as beautiful as living it?

💫 The Spark

The Sparkle Edit was born from a simple idea: why are the only stylish options for celebrating limited to weddings or corporate events? We wanted something different. A space for those who want to celebrate life—birthdays, proposals, special dinners, or “just because”—with beauty, intention, and no stress.

This was the third and final project of the bootcamp. I proposed the original idea, the name, and the business approach to the team, and led its development from start to finish: research, segmentation, strategy design, visual content, storytelling, automation, and performance tracking. It wasn’t just a case study—it was a way to apply everything we had learned, integrating creativity, technology, and conversion into a single ecosystem.

🧭 The Strategy

How do you connect with people looking for more than just a party?

We began with in-depth research on the market, competitors, messaging, and SEO using tools like Semrush, SimilarWeb, and Ubersuggest.

From there, we built a strategy based on three key pillars:

  1. Emotional segmentation: We created detailed and realistic buyer personas — Clara (the DIY dreamer), Emma (the expat celebrator), and Leo (the emotionally attuned digital nomad). Each with their own pace, values, and pain points.
  2. Non-linear conversion funnel: Every touchpoint (ad, blog, resource, email) was crafted to respond to a specific need — without forcing a rigid path.
  3. Connected ecosystem: Ads, content, lead magnets, and automations worked together to inspire, support, and convert.

My role was to lead and execute this entire structure — blending emotion and tech to create a personalized, measurable experience.

🌐 Website & Main Landing Page

We built our website on HubSpot, with a focus on trust, inspiration, and conversion. The structure combines an editorial-style homepage with testimonials, educational content, and well-placed CTAs — and a main landing page fully optimized to capture qualified leads.

This landing page features a form emotionally aligned with the brand voice. Once submitted, it automatically triggers:

  • Segmentation based on form responses
  • Personalized automation workflows via Make
  • Detailed tracking via Google Tag Manager and GA4

We manually connected the landing page to GTM, setting up custom tags, triggers, and variables to measure scrolls, CTA clicks, form submissions, and key funnel events. These are all visualized in Looker Studio dashboards for performance tracking by channel and customer profile.

🎯 Tailored Landing Pages per Persona

One generic landing page wouldn’t have worked. Each of our personas had distinct motivations, expectations, and funnel stages. Creating a custom page for Clara, Emma, and Leo allowed us to:

  • Connect emotionally from the very first moment
  • Tailor copy, visuals, and CTAs to their lifestyle and intent level
  • Trigger the right automation workflow through the correct form

This was key to boosting relevance, conversions, and segmentation — from the very first click.

📱 Social Media

Instagram, Pinterest, and TikTok were essential to meet each persona where they were — emotionally and digitally.

  • Instagram: Helped us nurture emotional connection with our audience through stories, reels, testimonials, and behind-the-scenes content.
  • Pinterest: Acted as a strong organic discovery channel. We built boards by celebration type and aesthetic, optimized with keywords and visuals. This drove top-of-funnel traffic to our blog and lead magnets.
  • TikTok: Acted as a channel for fast visibility and emotional conversion. We published inspiring videos, testimonials, and tools like the Sparkulator.

🔍 Evergreen and emotional SEO content:

Based on our research findings, I wrote all the blog articles, designed both to rank organically and to build emotional connection. Each article was integrated into the funnel, introduced free resources, and linked to premium ones. All content was visually adapted to social media and played a key role in attraction and conversion. The main articles included:

“How Much Does a Dream Party Really Cost?”

This piece broke the myth that a memorable party has to be expensive. It provided realistic price ranges and tips to optimize each budget item. It introduced the Sparkulator, which helped users prioritize based on style and budget.

“Top 5 Private Event Themes Trending in 2025”

A tour through five popular themes with visual inspiration and examples. This blog was connected to the Mini Lookbook, encouraging deeper exploration.

“Top 5 Features a Party Planning Kit Must Include”

Targeted at those looking to plan more structured events. It explained what a good kit should include and ended with a double invitation: download the free Ultimate Party Planning Checklist or get the Premium Party Planner Kit, a paid version with editable tools, Canva templates, and spreadsheets to guide the process. This post had a clear conversion goal.

“Our Favorite Hidden Gem Venues in Spain for Baby Showers, Anniversaries & More”

This article gathered unique, lesser-known venues, organized by event type and style. It reinforced Sparkle’s positioning as a relatable, authentic, and knowledgeable brand. It introduced the downloadable resource Hidden Gems: Venue Map.

📣 Ad Campaigns

Google Ads

I configured campaigns for each persona with emotional copy, custom extensions, and performance tracking through GA4, GTM, and UTMs.

Meta Ads

Creatives were tailored for each buyer persona, with warm visuals and direct copy, linking to emotional landing pages with persona-specific resources.

TikTok Ads

Native-style ads with aspirational tones (Leo) or fun/DIY approach (Clara). High visual appeal + simplified form for quick lead capture.

Promo Videos

I personally created all the AI-powered promo videos for each persona (script, voice, tone, and aesthetics) using InvideoAI:

  • Clara: budget-friendly 80s-themed party
  • Emma: intimate masquerade birthday
  • Leo: modern rooftop proposal

🎁 Relevant, attractive, and functional lead magnets

I designed a collection of downloadable resources, each with a specific function in the funnel:

  • The Mini Lookbook inspired early-stage users (TOFU) with aesthetic ideas, palettes, photos, and sensory text.
  • The Ultimate Party Planning Checklist provided structure for solo planners and acted as a gateway to the premium editable kit.
  • The Venue Map Lite offered a curated preview of venues, sparking desire and inviting users to the Pro version.
  • The Sparkulator, built in Canva, was an interactive calculator providing budget estimates and triggering automated sequences.

🤖 Email Automation & Segmentation

We built an automated system using Make and HubSpot to nurture leads from the first click.

Whenever a user submitted a form, the system automatically segmented them by persona and launched a Soap Opera-style email sequence, written with Gemini AI prompts tailored to each profile and resource downloaded.

Emails were sent via Outlook and followed a progressive narrative:

  • Welcome email aligned with the content requested
  • Emotional stories or examples by persona
  • Organic invitations to explore other resources or services

The system adjusted dynamically based on user behavior, creating a smooth and warm experience.

🤝 Collaborations

We developed a two-fold collaboration strategy: influencers for reach and social proof, and vendors for professional credibility and local synergy. I handled the full collaboration flow: initial segmentation (form), usage agreements, welcome kits, contact messages, and explanatory materials.

  • Influencers: We partnered with lifestyle, event, and expat micro-influencers (5K–50K followers) in Spain. They received access to our resources and showcased how they used them via Reels, TikToks, or Stories. Goal: increase organic reach, generate trust, and drive qualified traffic to lead magnets.
  • Vendors: We built strategic partnerships with local businesses (florists, photographers, decorators, venues, caterers, DJs…). Their collaboration boosted our authority, enhanced local SEO, and generated social content that inspired, educated, and converted. They were also part of our affiliate and cross-visibility income model.

💸 Budget and Performance

We designed a media plan with a €3,000/monthly budget:

  • Meta Ads: 40% of the budget was allocated to Meta Ads, focusing on emotional visibility and remarketing.
  • Google Ads: 25% was dedicated to Google Ads, aimed at capturing users with high intent.
  • TikTok Ads: 15% was set aside for TikTok Ads, leveraging visual impact and virality.
  • CRM/Email: 10% was used for CRM/Email efforts, emphasizing personalized and automated nurturing.
  • Influencers + A/B testing: The remaining 10% was invested in influencer partnerships and A/B testing to validate and scale successful content.

All actions were tracked with GA4, GTM, Meta Pixel, and Looker Studio dashboards. We monitored form submissions, scrolls, clicks, downloads, and automated flows, all connected by UTMs.

📊 Expected Results

Although this was a fictional project, we treated it as if it were real. We used AI and industry benchmarks to estimate the results this campaign could achieve in real conditions:

  • 2,773 qualified leads generated
  • 1,752 form submissions
  • 15–20% conversion rate to bookings or sales
  • Average ROAS of 3.42x on paid media
  • Average CPA of €34.51
  • +40% monthly increase in website visibility
  • 45% average open rate for automated emails

These projections were modeled using AI based on the defined structure, budget, and strategy. Results were integrated into interactive dashboards using GA4, GTM, HubSpot, and Looker Studio, allowing real-time optimization.

🧠 Key Learnings

This project allowed me to bring all my skills together into a realistic, connected strategy. I learned to:

  • Design flexible funnels by persona
  • Segment and nurture leads without losing warmth
  • Use GA4, GTM, and Looker Studio for data-driven decisions
  • Balance inspiring content with practical resources

Above all, I confirmed that personalization at scale is possible when strategy, tools, and storytelling work in sync.
