FlexFlow

Case Study

What if wellness could adapt to your daily chaos?

Context & Kick-off

FlexFlow was created with a clear mission: to bring yoga and pilates into everyday lives. Not a polished, idealised version — one that feels possible for people with tight schedules, endless responsibilities, and chaotic days.

This project aimed to craft a full-funnel outbound strategy to launch this fictional digital platform in a way that felt grounded, human, and emotionally resonant. As a group project, each team member brought their own strengths. My contribution focused on leading the research, defining the audience segmentation, building the strategic structure, mapping the campaign logic, and shaping an emotionally engaging narrative that could truly speak to people who feel disconnected from traditional wellness spaces.

📝 Who We Designed For

Our research began with a fundamental question: Who are we really trying to reach, and what's keeping them from showing up for themselves?

Through competitor analysis, keyword exploration, and empathetic listening, we identified three distinct audience segments and built detailed buyer personas for each:

Sophie

Overwhelmed Mom

Navigating a busy life with little time for self-care and a lot of internal guilt. Needs personal space without feeling selfish about it.

David

Burned-out Professional

Battling chronic stress and fatigue, seeking quick yet meaningful ways to feel better. Leading with physical pain and mental exhaustion.

Sara

Anxious Beginner

Intimidated by polished studios and rigid routines. Needs a safe, pressure-free way to start — without aesthetic expectations.

Our value proposition was built on a more grounded and powerful promise: wellness can adapt to you. No strict schedules. No aesthetic expectations. Just space to breathe, stretch, and reconnect — in your own way, on your own time.

🎯 Content Strategy: Building Trust, Not Just Funnels

We didn't build a traditional funnel — we built a trust ecosystem. For FlexFlow, the path to conversion wasn't linear: users could discover us through a Google search, an Instagram reel, or a local influencer's story. Each entry point had to feel like a warm invitation, not a hard sell.

Key content assets included:

  • SEO-driven blogs written in Gamma — titles like "How to Start Yoga at Home (Even if You're Super Busy)" and "3 Mindful Rituals When You Feel Overwhelmed" — designed to attract organic traffic and build early trust.
  • Lead magnets including the "Free Anti-Burnout Toolkit," gated behind trial signup — turning it into a meaningful entry point.
  • Social posts with phrases like "You don't need a studio to feel strong" — plus AI videos created with Hedra featuring diverse bodies and home-based wellness moments.
  • AI-powered video ads for YouTube (created with InvideoAI) — tailored micro-narratives for each persona that said, "We see you."
  • Instagram editorial plan with 4–5 weekly posts: 40% educational, 30% emotional wellness, 20% product-focused, 10% testimonials/UGC.
  • 7-day nurturing email sequence tailored by persona, guiding users gently through their trial experience.

Cross-channel examples: A Google Search ad for "gentle yoga for moms" linked to a postnatal pilates blog with a CTA to book a free class. An Instagram reel about "desk yoga in under 1 minute" directed users to the free trial landing. Day 3 of the trial included a personalised email featuring Sophie's story and a link to the Anti-Burnout Toolkit.

🚀 Campaign Activation

Google Ads & YouTube

We launched a structured outbound campaign built around search intent, divided into three ad groups by persona. Keywords included "yoga at home," "postnatal pilates," and "quick wellness workouts." All copy was written with empathy:

  • Sophie: "Try free for 7 days – Quiet yoga made for busy moms"
  • David: "Back pain? Burnout? Reset in 10 min. Start your free trial now"
  • Sara: "New to yoga? Zero pressure, step-by-step guidance. Try it free"

We complemented with Google Display retargeting campaigns and YouTube AI videos — authentic 30-second pieces that reflected real-life situations for each persona. All tracking was connected from HubSpot via GTM and GA4, with UTM tracking to monitor conversions by channel and audience, visualised in a Looker Studio dashboard.

YouTube Ads

We complemented search with YouTube AI videos — authentic 30-second pieces that reflected real-life situations for each persona.

Influencer Collaborations

We partnered with relatable micro-influencers (2K–15K followers) creating content for wellness, parenting, and physiotherapy — sourced via Shopify Collabs and manual Instagram/TikTok scouting. We offered an ethical, performance-based compensation model:

  • €5–10 per qualified lead
  • €50 bonus for 10+ conversions
  • Personalised affiliate link

Estimated reach: 200,000 users, contributing 100–150 qualified leads from this channel alone.

Technical Setup & Tracking

All tracking was connected from HubSpot via GTM and GA4 before the first ad went live — attribution built into the structure from day one.

Landing Page & Lead Magnet

The entire acquisition journey centred around a visually aligned landing page built in HubSpot, featuring an FAQ section, transparent privacy policy, the exclusive "Free Anti-Burnout Toolkit" gated behind trial signup, testimonials, and a strong CTA.

This page was connected to blog articles I wrote, such as "3 Mindful Rituals When You Feel Overwhelmed" and "How to Start Yoga at Home (Even if You're Super Busy)."

Personalised Automation (personal initiative)

After the group project ended, I independently designed a Make-based automation system that automatically identified each user's persona (Sophie, David, Sara), tagged leads within the CRM, and triggered tailored email routes. I connected HubSpot forms to GeminiAI and Outlook through Make, enabling auto-generated responses in an emotionally attuned tone — scaling personalisation while maintaining connection.

📏 Performance Tracking

We designed a comprehensive multi-channel measurement framework, with KPIs tailored to each channel, buyer persona, and content format. Main KPIs tracked:

  • Reach and engagement across Instagram and influencer collaborations
  • Click-through and conversion rates segmented by Google Ads group
  • Visits to blog articles, lead magnet downloads, and average time on page
  • Form submissions, emotional persona tagging, and funnel progression
  • Conversion journey: from free trial sign-up to active paid subscription

Tools used: Google Ads, GA4, GTM, Looker Studio, Meta Ads, HubSpot, Excel. All data centralised in a Looker Studio dashboard for real-time visibility.

🧠 Key Learnings

This project allowed me to build an outbound campaign from scratch, blending emotional insight, structure, and strategic thinking. I learned that effective segmentation goes far beyond demographics — it requires empathy and the ability to transform emotions into clear, actionable messaging.

One of the most valuable discoveries was understanding that the funnel is not always linear. I learned how to design a content ecosystem where each touchpoint — from a Reel or ad to an automation or nurturing email — could welcome users wherever they were in their journey.

Another key lesson was finding balance between creativity and analytics: creating Google and YouTube ads tailored to each persona while ensuring that emotional content was supported by robust tracking through GA4 and GTM.

After the group project ended, I took it further and built an automation flow in Make — this taught me how to scale human-centred personalisation using Make and GeminiAI, and to refine emotional messaging with the help of ChatGPT. When you truly understand who you're speaking to, everything clicks into place.
